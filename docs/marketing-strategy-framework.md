### Platform-Specific Guerrilla Tactics

#### Reddit
- **90/10 Rule:** 90% genuine contribution, 10% self-promotion
- Build karma in target subreddits first (2-4 weeks)
- Use throwaway accounts for product posts (link to main later)
- Post on weekday mornings (8-11am EST)

#### Product Hunt
- Launch Tuesday-Thursday for best visibility
- Only **10% of submissions get featured** (post-2024 algorithm)
- Build supporter list of 50+ people pre-launch
- First-hour momentum is critical (rank by upvotes/hour)

#### Hacker News
- Use "Show HN:" prefix for product launches
- Modest, technical language (avoid marketing speak)
- Respond to every comment within first hour
- Best times: weekdays 8-10am PST

### Micro-Influencer Collaboration

**Target Sizes:**
- **Nano:** 1K-10K followers (highest engagement, most authentic)
- **Micro:** 10K-100K followers (balance of reach + trust)

**Compensation Models:**
1. **Product Seeding:** Free lifetime access (best for new apps)
2. **Affiliate:** 20-30% commission on referred sales
3. **Revenue Share:** Ongoing percentage for long-term partnerships

### Viral Content Frameworks

#### WAVE Framework
- **W**ords (on-screen text hooks)
- **A**udio (trending sounds, voiceover)
- **V**isuals (before/after, demos)
- **E**motions (surprise, humor, satisfaction)

#### High-Performing Hooks
1. **Pattern Interruption:** "Stop scrolling if you [relatable pain]..."
2. **Before/After:** Show transformation in first 3 seconds
3. **Behind-the-Scenes:** "Building [feature] in real-time"
4. **Surprising Use Case:** "This app can do [unexpected thing]"
5. **Tutorial Marketing:** "How to [solve problem] in 30 seconds"

**Stats:**
- 63% of high-CTR videos hook in first 3 seconds
- Before/after works across ALL app categories
- 50% of consumers create UGC if brands provide guidance

## ðŸ› ï¸ Framework Methodology

### Step 1: Market Prioritization
Rank target markets by:
1. App Store revenue potential
2. iOS market share
3. Competition level
4. Localization complexity

**Output:** Clear recommendation on launch order.

### Step 2: ASO Strategy (Per Market)
For each target market:

1. **Keyword Research:**
   - Feature-based: "poop tracker", "bathroom log"
   - Benefit-based: "digestive health", "gut tracker"
   - Problem-solving: "IBS diary", "Crohn's app"
   - Competitor analysis: Top 5 competitors' keywords

2. **Metadata Optimization:**
   - App Name (30 chars): Brand + Primary Keyword
   - Subtitle (30 chars): Value prop + Secondary Keyword
   - Keyword Field (100 chars): 15-20 keywords, comma-separated

3. **Screenshot Strategy:**
   - Screenshot 1: Core value prop + key benefit
   - Screenshot 2: Most unique/viral feature
   - Screenshot 3: Social proof or gamification hook

### Step 3: Viral Content Playbook
Generate 10+ platform-specific content ideas:
- TikTok/Reels hooks (15-30 seconds)
- Tweet threads (educational/entertaining)
- Before/after transformations
- Behind-the-scenes dev content
- User testimonial templates

### Step 4: Guerrilla Tactics Roadmap

**Reddit:**
- Identify 3-5 target subreddits
- Karma-building strategy (2-4 weeks)
- Posting templates and timing

**Product Hunt:**
- Pre-launch timeline (4 weeks out)
- Supporter list building tactics
- Launch day playbook (first-hour focus)

**Micro-Influencer:**
- Target influencer profiles (nano vs micro)
- Outreach templates (personalized)
- Compensation models (product/affiliate/revenue share)

### Step 5: Localization Matrix
For each market:
- Messaging tone adjustments
- Cultural considerations (visuals, holidays, taboos)
- Translation requirements (native speaker vs machine)
- Key market differences vs US baseline

### Step 6: 7-Week Action Plan
Week-by-week roadmap with specific, actionable tasks:

**Weeks 1-2: Foundation**
- Build Reddit karma in target subs
- Set up social media profiles
- Competitor ASO research

**Weeks 3-4: Community & Content**
- Create Discord/Slack community
- Design first 3 screenshots
- Record 5-10 short-form videos
- Start influencer outreach

**Weeks 5-6: Launch Prep**
- Finalize Product Hunt assets
- Build supporter list (50+ people)
- Prepare press kit

**Week 7+: Launch Execution**
- Product Hunt launch (Tuesday-Thursday)
- Reddit posts (community first, product second)
- Influencer campaign activation
